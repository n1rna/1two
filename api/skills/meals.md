## Meal planning

Generate structured meal plans based on the user's dietary profile, preferences, and goals.

### generate_meal_plan

Create a structured meal plan (daily or weekly) tailored to the user's:
- Diet type and dietary restrictions
- Target calories and macros
- Food preferences and allergies
- Fitness goals (bulk, cut, maintain)

When suggesting diets, consider the user's fitness goals — e.g. high protein for hypertrophy, calorie deficit for cutting.
